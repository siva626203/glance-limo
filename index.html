FAQ

Added as a separate section around the preserved tour and transfer rules.

Are Singapore Transfer and Malaysia Transfer separate services?

Yes. Local Singapore transfer and cross-border Malaysia transfer should stay separate in navigation, forms and booking logic.

Do all Malaysia private tours require minimum 2 adults?

Yes. Malaysia tours are presented with a minimum 2 adults booking rule.

Do sit-in-coach tours show the same rule?

No. Shared seat-in-coach tours are priced per person with adult and child rates only.

Is there an overnight driver charge on Malaysia transfer?

Yes. For long distance journeys requiring overnight stay, driver accommodation must be covered at SGD50 per day.
