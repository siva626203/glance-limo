      1. Payment Terms

      • Full Payment Required: Full payment is required at the time of reservation unless you are an approved affiliate or corporate account with separate payment arrangements.
      • Payment Methods: Payments are processed via secure third-party payment gateways and approved payment methods.
      • Invoice and Confirmation: A booking confirmation and invoice will be sent to your registered email address upon successful payment.
      • Hourly / Disposal Services: Any usage beyond the booked duration will be rounded up to the next full hour.
      • Minimum Booking Duration Charge: The full booked duration will be charged regardless of actual usage. Unused hours are non-refundable and non-transferable.

      2. Cancellation Policy

      • Cancellations Within 72 Hours: Any cancellation made within 72 hours prior to the scheduled service time will be subject to a full charge, and all payments made will be forfeited.
      • Cancellations Before 72 Hours: Any cancellation made more than 72 hours before the scheduled service time will be eligible for a 50% refund.
      • Refund Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 7 business days.
      • No-Show: If the Client fails to appear at the pickup location without prior notice, the booking will be treated as a no-show and no refund will be given.

      3. Waiting Time Policy

      • Arrival Transfers (Airport Pickup): For airport arrival transfers, the chauffeur will wait for up to 1 hour from the actual flight landing time.
      • If the waiting time exceeds 1 hour, the chauffeur may continue waiting subject to the Client’s approval, and an additional 1-hour waiting charge will apply.
      • Departure Transfers / Point-to-Point Transfers: A complimentary waiting time of 20 minutes is included from the scheduled pickup time.
      • If the waiting time exceeds the initial 20 minutes and the chauffeur is instructed to continue waiting, the service will be charged as one additional transfer.
      • If the waiting time extends beyond 40 minutes and the chauffeur is still instructed to wait, the booking will be automatically converted to an hourly disposal service, subject to a minimum charge of 3 hours.
      • All extended waiting is subject to chauffeur availability and operational scheduling.

      4. Special Charges

      • Overnight Drive in Malaysia: A fixed charge of SGD150 will apply for chauffeur lodging and meals for overnight services in Malaysia.
      • Festive Periods: Published rates do not apply during festive seasons including Christmas and Lunar New Year.
      • Major Events: A 50% surcharge may apply during major events including Formula 1, Airshow, IMF World Bank Meetings, ASEAN Meetings, and similar high-demand events.
      • High-Demand / Peak Days: Additional surcharges may apply on selected high-demand dates.

      5. Reservations and Bookings

      • Provide accurate, complete, and up-to-date booking information.
      • Ensure the pickup date, time, address, flight details, and contact information provided are correct.
      • Be present and ready at the designated pickup location at the scheduled time.
      • Inform us promptly of any changes to your booking details.
      • Accept responsibility for delays, missed pickups, or service disruptions caused by inaccurate or incomplete information provided by you.

      6. Passenger Conduct

      • Treat our chauffeurs, staff, and vehicles with respect at all times.
      • No smoking, vaping, or consumption of alcohol inside the vehicle.
      • No eating or drinking inside the vehicle unless prior permission has been given, except for plain water.
      • Maintain cleanliness and avoid causing damage to the vehicle interior or exterior.
      • Comply with all applicable traffic laws, safety instructions, and seatbelt requirements during the journey.
      Glance Limousine reserves the right to refuse or terminate service without refund if any passenger engages in unsafe, abusive, unlawful, intoxicated, or inappropriate conduct.
